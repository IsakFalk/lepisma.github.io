Hi

Interests

My specific, doing-right-now, interests come and go in cycles and phases. I am broadly interested in computational sciences and the way they help in explaining physical and natural phenomena including human intelligence and group behavior. These days, I am into machine learning and stuffs including deep learning and evolutionary heuristics.

I am equally fascinated by data and simulation based exploration of complex systems. Its interesting how tiny interactions generate almost magical behavior. Check out a small exploration in small world networks here.
