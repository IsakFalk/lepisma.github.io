Hi

I am an undergraduate (final year) student majoring in Electrical Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology, Roorkee and am interested in general machine intelligence and learning (along with other things, see below).

Since you are here, you might be interested in checking out my blog posts. A few projects are open sourced here. Contact me via twitter or email me.

Interests

I am broadly interested in computational sciences and the way they help in explaining physical and natural phenomena including human intelligence and group behavior. Narrowing down, I am into Machine Learning methods including Deep Learning and Evolutionary heuristics.

I am equally fascinated by data and simulation based exploration of complex systems. Its interesting how tiny interactions generate almost magical behavior. Check out a small exploration in small world networks here.
